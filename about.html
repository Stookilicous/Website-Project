About Us

At Banano we strive to be the best. We want to give our customers a positive experience every time they come to one of our locations. We are a close community of staff and want our customers to not only see it but feel a part of it when they come in.

Banano uses real fruit and real frozen yogurt to ensure a supreme quality in each smoothie. We want our customers to know exactly what they are getting when they buy from us. We believe there shouldn’t be a complicated answer when asked what comes in a smoothie.

​Banano started as a small business from Billings MT, and has recently expanded to Boise ID.
